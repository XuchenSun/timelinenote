Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

TimelineNote is developed by Xuchen Sun. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All notes, categories, tags, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

iCloud Sync

If enabled, data syncs via Apple CloudKit. We cannot access your iCloud data.

AI Features (Optional)

Optional AI features require your own Moonshot AI (Kimi) API key. Content is sent to api.moonshot.cn for processing. Your API key is stored locally. We do not collect or access it.

Speech Recognition

Voice-to-text uses Apple's on-device Speech framework. Audio is not sent externally.

Camera & Photo Library

Used to attach images to notes. Photos are stored locally. We do not upload or share them.

Analytics & Tracking

We use no analytics, ads, or tracking. No usage data or personal information is collected.
